15 Nov 22

DRIVER:
left joystick: Y - forward speed
right joystick: X - turn speed
left bumper: auto rotate 180 deg left
right bumper: auto rotate 180 deg right
Y button: Slowmode

OPERATOR:
(speed of movement equal to right minus left)
right trigger: move rake up (positive)
left trigger: move rake down (negative)
X button: rotate rake to dispensing angle
Y button: rotate rake to starting angle
A button: rotate rake to ground-level angle
B button: rotate rake to just above ground level
back button: hold to run auto-dispense routine, release to cancel
start button: emergency release hardstop
